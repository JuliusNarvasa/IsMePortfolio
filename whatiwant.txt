Create a modern personal developer portfolio website.

* Clean, minimal, and personal
* Not corporate, not startup-marketing, not agency-style

* Professional but approachable
* Mobile-first responsive design

* Dark charcoal background
* Soft off-white text
* Single accent color (teal, cyan, or orange)
* Avoid bright gradients and excessive effects
